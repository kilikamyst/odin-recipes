Lasagna

Savor the Symphony: A Culinary Overture in Layers of Indulgence - The Artistry of Lasagna

Indulge your senses in the sumptuous layers of perfection that is lasagna. Picture tender sheets of pasta embracing a symphony of rich, savory meats, velvety ricotta, and a melody of melted cheeses. Each bite is a journey through a culinary masterpiece, where the robust flavors intertwine in a dance that captivates your palate.

The luscious tomato sauce, infused with aromatic herbs, weaves its way through this edible masterpiece, creating a harmony of taste that is both comforting and exhilarating. Lasagna: a timeless allure, a celebration of taste, and an invitation to savor the extraordinary.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Make sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make layers
  4. Go crazy with the cheese
  5. Bake it up!
